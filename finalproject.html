About

Can it be basketball season already?

After a competitive (albeit not very enjoyable) 2024-2025 campaign, the Deacs aim to get over the hump and return to the NCAA Tournament for the first time since the 2016-2017 season.

After graduating multi-year standouts like Hunter Sallis, Efton Reid, and Cameron Hildreth, this year’s squad features just four returning players: Tre’Von Spillers, Omaha Biliew, Marqus Marion, and Juke Harris. Promising transfers include Nate Calmese (Washington State), Myles Colvin (Purdue), Mekhi Mason (Washington), and Cooper Schwieger (Valparaiso).

Designed for fans both new and old, this site is intended to be a companion to follow the Demon Deacons’ basketball season. Features include a game schedule updated with results, a complete roster of players and coaches, and a metrics page to track the team’s resume for the 2026 NCAA tournament.

How will it all shake out? Stay tuned and we shall see. As always, Go Deacs!
