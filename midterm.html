About

The Friends of Palo Alto Library hosts a monthly sale featuring over 50,000 items available for purchase including used books, records, videos, magazines, and more. These items consist of donations from individuals, estates, and companies as well as items that the Palo Alto Library has removed from their shelves.

Items for sale are located in three primary locations: The Main Room, the Children's Room, and the Bargain Book rooms. The locations of these rooms as well as their hours, general contents, and pricing information are listed in the sections below.

Main Room

Location: The Main Room is located along the north driveway, adjacent to the Charleston Shopping Center.

Contents: General books

Pricing: Hardbound books start at $1, Paperbacks start at 50 cents

Rules & Procedures: Because of space limitations, only 160 customers are allowed into the Main Room at a time. Beginning at 8am on the Saturday of the sale, we hand out a maximum of two numbered tickets per person which allows customers to save their place in line until the room opens. Please note that if you miss the time your number is called, you will forfeit your place in line. Customers that don't arrive in time to receive a ticket will join the latter part of the line.

Restrictions: Once inside the Main Room, customers can select up to 12 items for purchase. This restriction will continue as long as a line remains outside. Customers that wish to purchase additional items must rejoin the remaining line outside.

Children's Room

Location: The Children's Room is located near the Cubberly Track Pavilion and the Greendell School.

Contents: Children's books, Picture books, school-age fiction and nonfiction books, non-English language books, books for parents and teachers, CDs, DVDs, and toys.

Pricing: Items start at 50 cents.

Rules, Procedures, and Restrictions: The Children's room opens one hour earlier than the Main room on Saturday. Customers are permitted to select no more than 12 books to purchase during this time.

Bargain Book Rooms

Location: Rooms K6 and K7

Contents: Discounted Books

Pricing: Hardbound books: $1, Paperbacks: 50 cents, Children's Books: 25 cents. On Sunday, all items are half-off. Additionally, customers may purchase a grocery bag for $5 which they can fill at their discretion to save even more.

Rules, Procedures, & Restrictions: The Bargain room opens one hour earlier than the Main room on Saturday.
